## Full Description

NIYYAH is a calm Qur'an understanding and reflection app for Muslim youth and young adults who want to connect with the Qur'an beyond recitation.

The app guides you one ayah at a time so you can slow down, read clearly, understand the meaning, and reflect with intention.

With NIYYAH, you can:

- Read Qur'an ayahs in a focused, distraction-free screen.
- View translation and source tafsir.
- Learn surah context and key lessons as content is reviewed.
- Reflect through guided prompts.
- Save journal entries and personal duas.
- Track completed ayahs and memorization progress.
- Save meaningful ayahs and vocabulary words for later review.

NIYYAH is not a fatwa app and does not replace qualified scholars or teachers. It is a learning and reflection companion designed to make Qur'an understanding feel calmer, clearer, and more approachable.
